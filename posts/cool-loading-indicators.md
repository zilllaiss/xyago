Well... maybe I'm a little late to the party since released on Github 10 years ago.
Not a new library and some of you might already know, 
but still... pretty cool, nonetheless.

The coolest thing about indicators that these don't use GIF or any image format at all, 
it's just pure text which animation by CSS!
That means less bandwith and less CPU cycles to render those animations.
Of course, if you have a bunch of them in one page (like right now),
you might already hear your fan spinning faster.
